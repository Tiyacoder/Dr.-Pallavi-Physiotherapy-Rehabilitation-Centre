Physio Myths vs Facts 🤔💡

Let's bust some common misconceptions!

❌ MYTH

"No pain, no gain" - you should push through pain during exercises

✅ FACT

Pain is your body's warning system! Good physiotherapy should challenge you but never cause sharp pain.

❌ MYTH

Physiotherapy is only for after injuries or surgery

✅ FACT

Prevention is better than cure! Regular physio can prevent injuries and optimize your performance.

❌ MYTH

You need to see a physiotherapist for months to see results

✅ FACT

While every case is different, many people start feeling better within the first few sessions!
